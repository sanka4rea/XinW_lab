Softwares

PAGnet

A online resource which construct a genomic integrated regulatory network based on chromatin immunoprecipitation coupled with high-throughput sequencing (ChIP-seq) and transcriptome profiling (RNA-seq).

https://compbio-cityuhk.shinyapps.io/pagnet/



DeepCC

A deep learning-based framework for cancer classification. https://cityuhk-compbio.github.io/DeepCC/


HTSanalyzeR2

A R pacakge for downstream analysis of high-throughput CRISPR/RNAi screens with interactive shiny report.

https://github.com/CityUHK-CompBio/HTSanalyzeR2

Tutorial website: https://htsanalyzer2.netlify.com/
